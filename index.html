Summer Weight Loss Plan

Daily Routine (with Isabghol)

  • Subah: 1 glass lemon honey water + light walk
  • Nashta: Chilled dahi + chia seeds + anda or besan chilla
  • Mid-morning: Tarbooz/kheera + 1 glass isabghol water
  • Lunch: Roti/rice + light sabzi + mint raita + salad
  • Evening: Chaas + seasonal fruit
  • Dinner: Light soup + salad (no heavy carbs)
  • Night: 1 glass cold milk with isabghol
